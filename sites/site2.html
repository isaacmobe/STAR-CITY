The Dark Forest

Founded: 9th Century

Population: Approximately 2,000 occupants during its peak.

History: The Dark Forest has been an enigmatic and mystical part of Star City since the 9th century. This dense and ancient forest was home to a community of druids who were known for their deep connection to nature and their mastery of herbal medicine. Over time, the forest became known for its eerie atmosphere and the legends of spirits that inhabit the woods.

Special Attractions:

  • The Enchanted Grove: A secluded area within the forest where ancient trees form natural archways.
  • The Druid's Circle: A stone circle used by the druids for ceremonies and rituals.
  • The Whispering Stream: A tranquil stream that is said to carry the whispers of the forest spirits.
  • The Hidden Caverns: A network of caves that were once used as shelter by the druids.

Site Coordinator: Lady Elara Greenwood

Lady Elara Greenwood is the current site coordinator for The Dark Forest. With a background in botany and a passion for folklore, Lady Elara brings a wealth of knowledge to her role. She is dedicated to preserving the mystical heritage of The Dark Forest and offers guided tours that delve into its rich history and legends.
