Green Trails Park

Founded: 1995

Area: 500 acres

History: Green Trails Park was established in 1995 as a conservation area and recreational park. It was created to preserve natural habitats while providing visitors with opportunities for outdoor activities and environmental education. The park has since become a beloved destination for nature enthusiasts and families seeking tranquility amidst green landscapes.

Special Attractions:

  • The Nature Trails: Scenic trails for hiking and birdwatching, offering encounters with diverse flora and fauna.
  • The Wildlife Viewing Areas: Designated spots for observing local wildlife in their natural habitats.
  • The Picnic Groves: Shaded areas equipped with picnic tables and facilities for family gatherings.
  • The Education Center: Interactive exhibits and programs focused on environmental conservation and sustainability.

Park Manager: Ms. Emily Collins

Ms. Emily Collins is the park manager of Green Trails Park. With a background in environmental science and a passion for conservation, Ms. Collins oversees park operations and community outreach programs. Her dedication ensures the park remains a pristine natural sanctuary and an educational resource for visitors of all ages.
